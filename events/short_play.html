PARACOSM - Short Play

All the world's a stage and most of us are desperately unrehearsed.

 

Charge up your brains and unleash the actor in you as you battle for the best theatre performance at VIBRANT 2020

Instructions

  • The time limit for the play is minimum-10 min. and maximum-20 min. Additional 5 minutes will be provided for preparation.
  • The script may be related to any topic and pertaining to any genre – horror, comedy, thriller, social, etc.
  • The script should be completely original. Plagiarism of any kind will not be tolerated.
  • Maximum number of people per team - 12
  • Minimum number of people per team - 4
  • The script has to be submitted before the deadline to confirm participation.
  • 3 hanging mics and 4 floor mics will be provided on stage.
  • Props and costumes will have to be managed by the participants themselves.
  • Prizes will be awarded to the best skit and the best actor.

 

Registration Details

  • Registrations will be done online.
  • All participants must have a Vibrant Del-Card.
  • Spot registrations will be subject to availability.
